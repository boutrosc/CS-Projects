**********
HOW TO USE
**********



Open your browser and visit http://www.wiki-flix.appspot.com

Once the page is loaded, you will be presented with a search bar and four tabs

To see what movies are currently playing in theaters, select the 'Now Playing' tab

To search for a movie or person, enter your search query and press enter
To view movies related to your search query, select the 'Movies' tab
To view people related to your search query, select the 'Persons' tab

To find a theater in your area, select the 'Find a Theater' tab
Once the page loads, allow the page access to your location
You will then be presented with movie theaters in your area

To return to the main page, select the Wiki-Flix logo
